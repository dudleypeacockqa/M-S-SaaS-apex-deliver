Design Direction Options

Three complementary lenses guide detailed mockups. Teams can mix elements as needed per surface.

A · Command Center

“Calm Control”

Dense dashboards with clear hierarchy. Indigo accents highlight priority data; right-rail keeps Copilot insights out of the main flow.

  • Use split-panel layouts with contextual rails.
  • Cards pair metrics with micro copy (“variance vs last week”).
  • Ideal for dashboards, pipeline, master admin console.
B · Narrative Workspace

“Guided Intelligence”

Document-like flow with anchored headers, inline AI callouts, and reviewer chips to build trust in valuations and Q&A responses.

  • Wide content area with typographic rhythm (56/32/20 px headers).
  • Copilot cards use gradient border + icon to indicate automation.
  • Best for valuation workbench, document rooms, Q&A.
C · Growth Surface

“Proof & Momentum”

Marketing + upgrade surfaces lean into social proof, ROI calculators, and sticky CTA bars that mirror in-product prompts.

  • Hero uses grid of testimonials + metrics; CTA gradient matches Primary palette.
  • Pricing + billing screens share the same badge language for tiers.
  • Applies to landing pages, blog, billing dashboard, upgrade modals.
